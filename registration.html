Quick Questions

How soon can we start?
Most families begin within 3-5 days of registration. Urgent requests can often be accommodated within 24-48 hours. Our Instant Tutoring platform is available for same-day help with homework or test prep.
What subjects do you cover?
We tutor all subjects from Kindergarten through Grade 12: Math (MTH1W to MCV4U), Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), English, French, Computer Science, IB & AP Programs, and EQAO/OSSLT test prep. We match tutors to your exact needs.
Is there a contract?
No long-term contracts. We offer flexible packages (10-session or 20-session discounts available) that fit your schedule and budget. You can pause or cancel anytime with 24 hours notice.
Can we switch tutors?
Absolutely. We want the perfect fit. If a tutor isn't right, we'll find one who is — no questions asked, no extra cost. Your child's comfort and learning style are our top priorities.
How much does tutoring cost?
Our rates are transparent: K-8 is $45/hour, Grades 9-10 are $55/hour, and Grades 11-12, IB & AP are $65/hour. We offer 10% off 10-session packages and 15% off 20-session packages. Your first assessment is always free. View full pricing details →
