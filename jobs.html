Tutor Position

Part-time / Contract — Brampton, Caledon, Bolton & Online

📋 Responsibilities

  • Provide one-on-one and group tutoring sessions to students in various subjects, including math, science, English, and more
  • Assess students' strengths and weaknesses to develop tailored learning plans
  • Create engaging and interactive lessons to promote student understanding and engagement
  • Monitor student progress and provide regular feedback to students and parents/guardians
  • Collaborate with the tutoring center team to develop and implement effective teaching strategies
  • Maintain a positive and supportive learning environment for students

🎓 Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Education, or related field (preferred)
  • Previous tutoring or teaching experience is an asset
  • Strong knowledge and understanding of the subject matter
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Patient, adaptable, and able to work effectively with students of diverse backgrounds and learning styles
  • Legally authorized to work in Canada

✨ What We Offer

  • Ongoing professional development opportunities to enhance your teaching skills
  • Flexible schedules that work around your commitments
  • Competitive compensation with performance incentives
  • A supportive team environment with mentorship from experienced educators
  • Opportunity to make a real difference in students' academic journeys

Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifications do I need?

A strong background in your subject area is essential. Teaching or tutoring experience is preferred but not required — we value passion and dedication.

How much do tutors earn?

We offer competitive rates that reflect your expertise and experience. Base pay starts at $30/hour with opportunities for bonuses and increases.

Is this a remote position?

We offer both in-person and online tutoring. You can choose the format that works best for you.

How do I apply?

Send your resume and cover letter to info@thestudyzone.ca with "Tutor Application" in the subject line.

Ready to Apply?

We'd love to hear from you. Please send your resume and a cover letter outlining your experience and passion for tutoring.

📧 info@thestudyzone.ca

Please include "Tutor Application" in your subject line.

⚠️ Important Note

We have a strict policy in place to select the best tutors. Please DO NOT call. Submit your application via email only, and we will follow up if you meet our selection criteria. We review every application carefully and appreciate your patience.
