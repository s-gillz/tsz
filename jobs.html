Ontario-Certified Tutor

📍 Brampton, Caledon, Bolton & Online ⏱️ Part-time / Contract 💵 $45 - $75 / hour

📋 Responsibilities

  • Provide one-on-one and small group tutoring sessions to students in various subjects (Math, Science, English, etc.)
  • Assess students' strengths and weaknesses to develop tailored, concept-based learning plans
  • Create engaging and interactive lessons to promote deep understanding and student engagement
  • Monitor student progress and provide regular, constructive feedback to students and parents
  • Collaborate with the TSZ team to develop and implement effective teaching strategies
  • Maintain a positive, supportive, and distraction-free learning environment

🎓 Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Education, or a related field (Ontario Certified Teachers highly preferred)
  • Previous tutoring or teaching experience is a strong asset
  • Strong knowledge and understanding of the Ontario curriculum (MTH1W to MCV4U, Sciences, English)
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Patient, adaptable, and able to work effectively with students of diverse backgrounds and learning styles
  • Legally authorized to work in Canada
  • Must pass a Vulnerable Sector Criminal Background Check

✨ What We Offer

  • Ongoing professional development opportunities to enhance your teaching skills
  • Flexible schedules that work around your personal commitments
  • Competitive compensation with performance incentives
  • A supportive team environment with mentorship from experienced educators (like our founder, S. Gill)
  • The opportunity to make a real, measurable difference in students' academic journeys
