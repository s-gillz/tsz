Building Essential Skills

Core Competencies for Early Success

Our program uses a "Whole-Child" approach, blending play-based learning with structured lessons to develop the core competencies required for the Ontario Kindergarten curriculum.

  • Early Literacy & Numeracy: Letter recognition, phonics, and foundational number sense
  • Cognitive Development: Problem-solving, critical thinking, and memory skills
  • Physical Milestones: Fine motor skills (writing/drawing) and gross motor skills
  • Communication: Building language proficiency and social-emotional confidence
  • Independence: Fostering self-reliance and the "soft skills" needed for a classroom setting
  • Social-Emotional Skills: Sharing, taking turns, following instructions, and making friends
