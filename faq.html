Pricing & Packages

How much does tutoring cost at The Study Zone?
Our rates are competitive, transparent, and based on grade level:
  • Grades K-8: $45/hour
  • Grades 9-10: $55/hour
  • Grades 11-12, IB & AP: $65/hour

We also offer package discounts: 10% off for 10-session packages, and 15% off for 20-session packages. Your first academic assessment is always completely free.

What is your cancellation policy?
We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice may be charged at 50%. We understand emergencies happen and handle them on a case-by-case basis. You can easily reschedule through your parent portal or by calling us.

Subjects & Curriculum

What subjects and grades do you tutor?
We tutor all subjects from Kindergarten through Grade 12, fully aligned with the Ontario curriculum:
  • Math: MTH1W, MPM2D, MCR3U, MBF3C, MHF4U, MCV4U, MDM4U
  • Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Language: English & French
  • Specialized: Computer Science & Coding, IB & AP Programs
Do you help with EQAO and standardized test prep?
Yes. We offer specialized, data-driven preparation for:
  • EQAO (Grades 3, 6, 9)
  • OSSLT (Ontario Secondary School Literacy Test)
  • SAT & ACT
  • IB Internal Assessments (IAs)
Our tutors are intimately familiar with Ontario curriculum standards and test formats, helping students build confidence and achieve their best scores.
Do you offer summer tutoring programs?
Yes! Our Summer Tutoring Program is designed to prevent the "summer slide" and prepare students for the upcoming school year. We offer:
  • Flexible scheduling (1-5 days/week)
  • Intensive catch-up programs for struggling students
  • Preview courses for advanced topics
  • IB & AP prep intensives

Scheduling & Logistics

Do you offer in-home tutoring in Brampton and Caledon?
Absolutely. We provide premium in-home tutoring throughout Brampton, Caledon, Bolton, and the broader GTA. Our tutors come directly to your home at a time that works for your family, providing a comfortable, distraction-free environment. We also offer live online tutoring for students anywhere in Canada and beyond.
How soon can tutoring sessions start?
Most families begin within 3-5 days of registration. For urgent needs (like an exam next week), we can often arrange a tutor within 24-48 hours. For immediate, same-day help with homework, check out our Instant Tutoring platform.
Can we switch tutors if it's not a good fit?
Absolutely. We want the perfect match. If your child isn't connecting with their tutor, we'll find a better fit at no extra cost. Your child's comfort and learning style are our top priorities. We have over 30 vetted tutors across the GTA to ensure we find the right personality and teaching style for your child.

Tutors & Quality

How do I know if my child needs a tutor?
Common signs your child may benefit from tutoring include:
  • Declining grades or report card concerns
  • Frustration, anxiety, or avoidance of homework
  • Poor test performance despite studying
  • Falling behind classmates
  • Lack of confidence in specific subjects
Not sure? Our free academic assessment helps identify specific gaps and creates a personalized learning plan — with zero obligation.
Are your tutors qualified and background-checked?
Yes, safety and quality are our top priorities. All tutors are Ontario-certified teachers or university graduates with deep subject expertise. Every single tutor undergoes:
  • A criminal background check (Vulnerable Sector Screening)
  • Strict reference verification
  • Our proprietary pedagogical training program
  • Ongoing professional development
